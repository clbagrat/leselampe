We're all, one way or another, learning German. This year I decided to take it seriously and even built a special app for myself. I really liked it, and I want to share it with you.

How it works

The main idea is that you read entertaining, level-appropriate content and gradually encounter new words and reinforce them in context. To do that, the app supports two use cases:

Story generation and RSS feed

Story generation

Generate your own stories, choose the language level, length, and style, set a prompt, and start reading.

You don't have to invent a story hook yourself - just use the suggest button.

RSS feed

Read current news or articles from your favorite publications via RSS.

You don't need to know the RSS link - just type the site address into search.

Any RSS subscription can be adapted to your level.

Feeds in any language are supported.
